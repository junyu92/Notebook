# 间接引语(reported speech)

## 间接疑问句(reported question)

可以用**ask**, **wonder**, **want to know**之类的动词来转述疑问句.

### Wh-疑问句

直接引语和间接引语中的wh-疑问句都有**when**, **what**, **which**, **how**之类的词

* **When** did you start acting, Melissa?(直接引语)
* **Guy** asked **Melissa** when she started acting.(间接引语)

* **How** can we find out?
** I was **wondering how** we can find out.

### yes/no疑问句

用**if**/**whether**.

* Has the taxi arrived yet?
* Someone was **wondering if/whether** the taxi has arrived yet.

### **在间接疑问句中, 主语像在陈述句中那样位于动词之前.**

### 索要信息

为了礼貌地索要信息, 有时在**Could you tell me ...?**或**Do you know ...?**之类的短语之后用一个间接疑问句.

* **Could you tell me** what time the concert starts?

### 时态变化

在间接引语中, 常有时态、代词等的变化.

* We asked what the problem **was**.
* Tom asked if Nick **needed** a gift.
